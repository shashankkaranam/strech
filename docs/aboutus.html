FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our Feed a Meal initiative and how to get involved.

How does it work?

Our Feed a Meal initiative connects donors with those in need. You can choose to donate meals as an individual or through a restaurant. Simply follow the prompts on our donation page to get started.

Who can donate?

Anyone can donate meals! Whether you are an individual, a restaurant, or a local business, your contribution is valuable. Join us in making a difference in our community.

What types of meals?

We accept a variety of meals, including prepared dishes, packaged foods, and fresh produce. All donations should be safe for consumption and adhere to health guidelines. Check our website for specific requirements.

How are meals delivered?

Meals are delivered by our dedicated volunteers who ensure that food reaches those in need promptly. You can also choose to volunteer for deliveries if you're interested. Your support makes a significant impact!

Can I volunteer?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers to help with meal deliveries. Sign up on our website to join our team and make a difference in your community.
